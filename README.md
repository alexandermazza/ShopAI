## 🚀 What is ShopAI?

ShopAI revolutionizes the online shopping experience by bringing **conversational AI** directly to your Shopify product pages. Customers can ask natural language questions about your products and receive instant, intelligent answers based on your product information and store policies.

**No more lost sales due to unanswered questions. No more overwhelming customers with endless review scrolling.**

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🗣️ **Ask Me Anything - AI Product Q&A**
- **Natural Conversations**: Customers ask questions in plain language
- **Intelligent Responses**: AI answers using your product details, reviews, and store policies
- **Store Context Aware**: Includes shipping, returns, store hours, and policies in responses
- **Multi-language Support**: Responds in 8+ languages
- **Smart Suggestions**: AI generates relevant questions customers might have

### 📊 **AI Review Summarization**
- **Instant Insights**: Summarizes dozens of reviews into concise highlights
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Identifies key praise and concerns from customer feedback
- **Time-Saving**: Helps customers make decisions faster
- **Customizable Tone**: Professional, friendly, or enthusiastic summaries

### 🎨 **Seamless Shopify Integration**
- **Theme App Extension**: Add to any theme with zero coding
- **Drag & Drop**: Install blocks through Shopify's theme editor
- **Fully Customizable**: Match your brand colors and style
- **Mobile Optimized**: Beautiful experience on all devices
- **Lightning Fast**: Optimized for performance and SEO

## 🚀 Getting Started

### **1. Install the App**
Visit the [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/) and search for "ShopAI"

### **2. Set Up Your Store Information**
- Add your shipping and return policies
- Include store hours and contact information
- Customize the AI's tone and personality

### **3. Add to Your Product Pages**
- Go to your theme editor
- Add the "Ask Me Anything" block
- Optionally add the "Review Summarizer" block

### **4. Customize & Launch**
- Style the blocks to match your brand
- Test with sample questions
- Go live and watch engagement soar!
